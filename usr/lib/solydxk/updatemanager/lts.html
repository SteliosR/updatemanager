SolydXK Business Edition (BE)

The business editions of SolydXK follow Debian stable, Debian security, and the SolydXK LTS (Long Term Support) repository.

SolydXK, and Debian only publishes updates that either have low IT risk or that reduce IT risk.
These updates are all tested by the SolydXK, and Debian maintainers, the testing team, and in the field through the regular SolydXK Home editions, thus reducing the risk of software regression.
