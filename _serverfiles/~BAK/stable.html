SolydXK Business Edition (BE/BO) - 2014.07.15

Changes

  • Update Manager
    A system maintenance section was added to the UM main window, but because of its potential danger of breaking your system it has been disabled by default. You can enable maintenance in the preferences window.

Update information

Read this whether you update with the Update Manager, or terminal.

  • Configuration files
    You might be asked to replace certain configuration files. It is recommended to keep the currently installed file (default selected).

 

The business editions of SolydXK follow Debian stable, Debian security, and the SolydXK Business repository.

SolydXK, and Debian only publishes updates that either have low IT risk or that reduce IT risk.
These updates are all tested by the SolydXK, and Debian maintainers, the testing team, and in the field through the regular SolydXK Home editions, thus reducing the risk of software regression.
